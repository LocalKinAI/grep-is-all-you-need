# ben_cao_gang_mu — 核心概念

## 主题
Li Shizhen's 16th-century *Compendium of Materia Medica* is the greatest pharmacological work in Chinese history, systematizing 1,892 medicinal substances through 27 years of research, observation, and error-correction—establishing the foundation for traditional Chinese medicine pharmacology.

## 核心概念

- **四气 (Four Qi)**: Temperature classification (cold/cool/neutral/warm/hot) determining a substance's fundamental action. "Cold treats heat conditions; warm treats cold conditions."

- **五味 (Five Flavors)**: Taste-based organ and action mapping (sour=liver/astringent, bitter=heart/drying, sweet=spleen/tonifying, pungent=lung/dispersing, salty=kidney/draining).

- **配伍 (Combining Principles)**: Seven herb relationship types governing safe and effective combinations; the Eighteen Antagonisms and Nineteen Incompatibilities specify dangerous pairings still used clinically today.

- **发明 (Personal Discovery)**: Li's own experimental observations and clinical tests—distinguishing verified knowledge from inherited tradition. "Wherever there was doubt, I personally investigated."

- **炮制 (Preparation Method)**: Processing fundamentally transforms herb properties (raw vs. honey-fried vs. wine-steamed). "The same herb processed differently has different effects."

- **辨证论治 (Pattern Differentiation First)**: Identify constitutional pattern (cold/hot, deficiency/excess) before selecting herbs—symptoms follow pattern, not vice versa.

- **本草 (Materia Medica)**: Comprehensive natural history spanning water, fire, earth, minerals, plants, animals, and human-derived substances—unified under medicinal function rather than taxonomy.

## 关键引文

> "Among all herbs that supplement the five organs and calm the spirit, none surpasses ginseng." — Ren Shen (Ginseng) entry

> "I have combed through more than 800 works... wherever there was doubt, I personally investigated—going to the mountains, fields, and markets—and recording only what I verified." — Li Shizhen's method statement

> "The older the peel, the better... must be at least 3 years old; 10+ years is superior." — Chen Pi (Aged Tangerine Peel) entry, on preparation value

> "Small doses move blood; larger doses purge the bowels; dose determines function." — Da Huang (Rhubarb) entry, on dosing wisdom

## 实践要点

- **Constitution before symptoms**: Pattern identification precedes herb selection
- **Processing is medicine**: Same substance has different effects based on preparation method
- **Formula is the unit**: Individual herbs combine into emergent therapeutic effects; relationships matter as much as ingredients
- **Test, don't assume**: Correct prior errors through direct observation and personal verification
- **Food-medicine boundary is permeable**: Dietary recommendations integrate with pharmacological treatment (藥食同源)
